About Us

Mentally VR is a platform where users will immerse themselves in an enviroment where they feel at peace, can organize their thoughts and have their therapy whenever they are ready to speak and talk-about-it.

We know how hard it is to have to identify, then to accept that we have a problem, nobody wants them. However, everyone has them, and we have to deal with it. Then we have to also think about who and how to seek for help, it's like having an obstacle after another. We are here to help you, not having the pressure or fear of having to show yourself while you take care of your mental health. We want you to be and feel comfortable from your own home.

Vision
Vision
We see a world where the stigma around Mental Health is debunked, for that we are here to bring a platform for those that need a hand and when they feel more confident about themselves help others and speak up. This is everyone's job to get to that goal. We are proud to be part of this movement.
Why We Care
We have had our mental health problems and looked for ways to get help, we know that being in the right place, with the right person can make a happier you, happier us
